About Gulabi Gala

Welcome to Gulabi Gala, a vibrant celebration of culture, music, and togetherness! Join us on April 1st, 2nd, and 3rd, 2025, for an unforgettable experience filled with entertainment, creativity, and joyful moments. With a proven track record of organizing successful events, we have hosted spectacular programs in Kaloor, Kochi, and Kottakkunnu, Malappuram. Our passion for curating unique and engaging experiences has made Gulabi Gala a much-awaited annual fest, bringing people together to celebrate in a lively and colorful atmosphere.

This year, Gulabi Gala is proud to present an exciting Flea Market featuring 120+ stalls and 10 food courts. Our main vision is to provide an opportunity for individuals who sell their products online from home, giving them a platform to showcase their creations to a wider audience. We believe in empowering small businesses and fostering a community of creativity and entrepreneurship. From electrifying performances to interactive sessions, Gulabi Gala promises something for everyone. Whether you're a music lover, an art enthusiast, or simply looking for a great time, this is the place to be!

Stay tuned for more updates, and get ready to immerse yourself in the magic of Gulabi Gala 2025 !
